### üéØ Data Literacy Lesson #3: IP Addresses

**KASTOR**: "Let me explain what we just found."

**IP Address = Internet address**
- Think of it like your home address, but for computers
- Every device that connects to the internet gets one
- 203.0.113.45 is the address that both edited Shadow AND played the game

**Device Fingerprint = Digital fingerprint**
- A unique ID for each device (phone, computer, etc.)
- Like how your thumbprint is unique to you
- DFP:7a9c... is Kaito's phone's "fingerprint"

**DETECTIVE**: "So the same phone was used to change Shadow and then play as Noctis?"
